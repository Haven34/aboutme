About Me

My name is Cole Weathers and I am so glad you have come to my Photography site. I am 26 years old and have done Photography for 12 years. I enjoy reading, writing, and being with my family and pets. I Love the outside and all the calm and comfort it brings me. The equipment I use is a Canon EOS Rebel T7 DSLR camera and kit. I have gotten a ton of great shots with this camera and I don't plan to stop anytime soon. My rates vary from what is asked of me but I do not charge per picture. Once again, my hours vary since photo shoots can happen middle of the day, middle of the night, and in between so the prices and the shoots have to be discussed by email, call, and text. Types of events I do include Weddings, Family photo shoots, and much more. I will do just about anything.
